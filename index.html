SPORTS

Gymnasium

There is a separate gym with the newest equipment for men and women. A modern day gymnasium (as gym used to be called way back in Ancient Greece) is a place for indoor physical workout where various equipment and machines are typically used. For some people, a typical gym is a place where you focus on weight lifting and similar activities.

Swimming pool

A swimming pool of exceptionally high standards for all seasons. Members can use separate swimming pools based on gender. The filter plant has been put in place, and the pool has a decent roof over it. There are also gas heaters available.

Tennis courts

There are eight grass tennis courts of international standards and one hard court with the most modern flood lighting available. There to offer their services are markers and pickers. There are several tournaments going on, but one that stands out is the annual All Over UK Governor's Cup Tennis Tournament.
