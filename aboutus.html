About Us

ZX club is spread over a vast land. The land of the club is 300000 Sq Feet. The club was founded in 1910 and since then has been supporting social, cultural and sports activities in the region for more than 100 years. It has around 3500 members. It is located in the very heart of the city. It is at a distance of 01 km from the metro station and 13 km from the airport. When it comes to the level of excellence and adequacy required by international standards, ZX Club meets all the required standards measured by any yardstick. It can be confidently vouched for as a symbol of high social order.
